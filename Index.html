About Me/Artist Statment

Hi, all my name is Lee. I'm a New Media, and Metalsmithing artist from a small town in south
Texas called Brenham, If you know about blue bell Ice cream that's where its from. I like to push
art to the extreme and just try to have fun while trying out completely different and new things.
In my art journey so far I have also become very fond of screen printing as well. I'm currently in my
junior year of college and hope to design video game models when I graduate. Some of my
favorite projects I've made are a 3D Modeled Castle, and a life sized trident.
